EFG encourages you to reflect on three questions which are the pillars on which we build our practice.

How will you benefit from a Growth Partner?


Not all Partnerships entail working with people who are growth mindset practitioners. Having delivered growth in multiple environments, our teams relish engaging in the following.

• Growth opportunities

Growth opportunities often are within arm’s reach of businesses and can be unlocked with the right insights, provided team are willing to revisit fundamental assumptions and challenge status quo. Cross industry lessons also prove insightful as existing teams and encouraged to look for new ways to unlock potential. Innovations and new approaches often need not be very expensive exercises,and we can help.

• Market research and Analysis

Market research and Analysis helps Businesses uncover strategies for growth and for key trends impacting an industry and allows teams to understand what is and isn’t working for rivals. Good and insightful analytics coupled with sold grassroot experience helps uncover key information nuggets that can shape strategies, messaging, and positioning, and we can help.

• Business expansion

Business Development and strategic growth planning including Merger and Acquisition consulting and implementation, and/ or post M&A integration can transform your business. We can help with that, or to flesh out a fully-formed market entry strategy for your export business, and even formulate capital planning and budgeting for your international expansion.
