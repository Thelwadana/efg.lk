Nivran Weerakoon

Co-Founder and Managing Director, Business Leader,
Board Member, Catalyst

Nivran is a Fellow Member of the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants (UK), along with an MBA from the University of West London (UK).

Nivran has held several senior leadership roles including being the Chief Financial Officer of Lanka Bell Limited at the age of 23. Nivran’s present engagements comprise the active roles he plays as Managing Director at Media Monkey (Pvt) Ltd.

a boutique advertising agency in Sri Lanka and as Director/CEO of Global Conversions FZ-LLZ an automobile assembly operation, which he co-founded in the UAE. Nivran presently serves as a Directors on the Boards of Royal Ceramics Lanka PLC, Delmege Limited (Holding Company of Delmege Group), Fly Lankan Asia (Pvt) Ltd., Kasper Global (Pvt) Ltd., Idea Propulsion Labs Pte. Ltd (Singapore), William Angliss Institute at SLIIT, Child Action Lanka and has functioned as the Vice Chairman of Lanka Electricity (Pvt) Ltd. Nivran’s experience in large listed, private and public enterprises has exposed him to dynamic business environments through which responsiveness to constant change is a necessity. This has sharpened his skillset to provide strategic leadership and contribute to the effective execution of strategies identified thereby enabling him to provide value to organizations, through his collective experience.
