Sriyan de Silva Wijeyeratne

Sriyan de Silva Wijeyeratne

Co-founder and Executive Director- EFG Business Transformation Practitioner, Consultant,
Corporate Directorand Social Catalyst

Sriyan possesses a First-Class Degree in Business Administration and a Masters Degree from the University of Sri Jayawardenapura. He is a Fellow member of CIMA, UK, and is also a CGMA. Sriyan has held several leading Corporate and other roles across his multi-faceted career spanning 30 years. He played transformative roles as the MD/ CEO of Teejay Lanka PLC, which surged to become one of the S&P Top 20 Companies during his time and was recognized as a Winner of Forbes “Asia’s 200 Best under a Billion Dollar Company”. He was also the Managing Director of Hemas Consumer Business. As Country Manager of Microsoft for nine years, he established their footprint in Sri Lanka whilst building many initiatives of national significance and winning many awards together with his team. Sriyan later ran the Microsoft business for Bangladesh and Nepal, as well. Sriyan was also the Chairman/CEO of the Employees Trust Fund Board, a Marketing Director in Telecommunication, a Group CEO in Consumer Electronics and managed Brands within FMCG companies, with Nestles.

Sriyan has several awards to his credit, had Executive education at many global institutions including INSEAD and CCL, and is a frequent speaker at leading forums locally and globally. He was previously named CIMA Business Manager of the Year and he was listed on the CIMA Top 50 Business Icons in 2021.

Sriyan’s decision making is deeply rooted on values and transparency, and he is engaged with startup operations and providing Consulting support to businesses that are hungry for growth and transformation.
