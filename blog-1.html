Dr. Nuwan Wimalana

Dr. Nuwan Wimalana

Chairman and Business Consultant, Corporate Director, Visiting Lecturer

Dr. Wimalana holds a Doctorate in Business Administration (with a marketing specialization) from the University of Kelaniya, an MBA from UK and a EDBA from the University of Colombo. He had his executive Education at NUS Singapore, IIM India, Cambridge Business School England and Griffith Australia.

Dr. Nuwan Wimalana is a Corporate Leader in the Sri Lankan business arena with over 40 years of experience. He worked for over 25 years with Nestle and Coca Cola in Sri Lanka, Malaysia and Nepal holding Senior Management Positions in Sales and Marketing. He has 12 years of experience as a Business Head including 7 years as the CEO of Swadeshi Industrial Works PLC and 5 years as Director/Group CEO of Delmege Forsyth & Co.; a Conglomerate with 10 diversified business clusters.

Dr. Nuwan also worked as the Director, Business Development at Atlas Axillia and then engaged himself as a Business Consultant for many small and medium enterprises whilst sharing his knowledge as a visiting lecturer to many local and international Universities in Management and Marketing
He serves as a Member of the Education Reforms Committee of SLIM, the President of Detergent Manufacturers Association of Sri Lanka and a member of the Subject Benchmark panel for Marketing of the University Grant Commission of Sri Lanka.
