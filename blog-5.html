Deshika Rodrigo

Director Leadership and Executive Life Coach,
HR Consultant and Diversity Specialist

Deshika holds a BA Honors Degree in English and Psychology from the University of Washington, USA, an MBA from the Australian Institute of Business, is an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Bankers, UK, a Chartered Member of the Chartered Institute of Personnel and Development (CIPD) UK, a certified Executive and Strengths Coach and an accredited Practitioner of MBTI and DiSC Psychometric tools.
Deshika Rodrigo has over 25 years of experience in HR including Microsoft Corporation, USA and, most recently, at the Standard Chartered Bank, Sri Lanka where she was the Head of Human Resources, for 11 years. She is a guest lecturer at several institutes and has been invited to speak at various forums.
Deshika specializes in Executive Coaching and Development, coaching through Career transitions, Assessment Centers, the design and implementation of HR policies, enhancing employee engagement, professional etiquette, Women’s Empowerment and the use of psychometric tools to develop leadership, self-awareness and teamwork.
Deshika is the recipient of the 100 Most Influential Global HR Professionals award and Sri Lanka’s Top Women Leaders award (by the World Federation of HR Professionals & the HRD Congress – 2017)
