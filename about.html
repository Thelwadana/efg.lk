ABOUT US

Eye For Growth (Pvt) Ltd, or EFG, is a boutique Consulting and Services entity, which brings together a unique blend of experienced, exposed, and successful resource personnel, combined with an ethos of a youthful drive, ambitious aspirations, and a challenger mindset. We seek to explore the multi-faceted dimensions of Growth, exploring unseen vistas, unchartered territories and new strategies, in a quest to support our clients on their respective journeys towards success.

Undertaking a range of assignments under the pillars of Organizational Growth, Business Transformation and Optimization, and Capital Strategy, EFG seeks to understand the unique situations and challenges faced by businesses and present modern breakthrough answers through meaningful and deep engagements with our clients. EFG appreciates that every challenge will require fresh thoughts, transformation, and new answers amidst some of our most turbulent times. Our team seeks to improve the effectiveness and efficiency of each client’s business through our work.

Recent in history but rich in individual maturity, credibility, and business success, we come together with our external resources and extensive networks, to become a solid asset and business partner for any organization. Our small sized team with its innovative, welcoming, and flexible culture looks to engage selective clients on long term relationships which bring mutual value and tangible results.

HOW CAN EFG HELP ?

We are here to help each client transform and grow their business, with a collaborative approach towards each strategic decision, with a clear predefined outcome in mind. At Eye for Growth (EFG) our approach is centered around strategically driven processes aimed at driving business growth. Our highly skilled team of Directors and Consultants work with the business teams to jointly craft efficient strategies guided by a robust analysis of the current situation, market conditions, data analytics and an in-depth understanding of requirements and company culture.

We aim to deliver tangible value through our business transformation efforts that adopt a prudent and methodological approach to consolidate every element that supports an entity’s business growth, in line with their own goals and ambitions. We also look to educate teams on areas of technology, sustainability etc.








What drives the need for EFG’s services?

Often a team of expert consultants with a rich pool of collective experience is sourced by entities as a response to shifting paradigms in the market, an eroding competitive edge, or declining financial returns. We believe that a growth focused, outside-in approach towards transformation strategy, brought about by people with solid demonstrated track records of success, in conjunction with the business team’s insights, can help entities improve overall business operations and streamline processes.

The ultimate outcome for you would be efficiency improvement, better competitiveness, and unlocking profitability and growth. We are on a journey beyond the mandate of setting strategy and will work with organizations to ensure a continuous commitment to improve performance and work towards establishing a growth culture within the organization.






What’s our approach?

As part of our approach, we carry out a comprehensive business review, which follows through to a problem diagnostic assessment. This can lead to strategy setting or fine tuning, which may extend to re-engineering of existing processes and implementing strategies that enable top-line growth and other shifts. According to the Harvard Business Review, “A well-defined problem leads to a breakthrough solution”.

Our collective experience of over 10 decades amongst our directors is sought to be coupled with the Business team’s exposure and insights, to help ensure that your business can excel in its defined space with strategic, yet practical, solutions to ensure that each business harnesses its full growth potential. We do not approach companies with standard models and templates, and each assignment is a customized bottom-up exercise, where the appropriate tools and frameworks will be leveraged.









Businesses CAN sustain what is newly created

Even the best solution needs continuous reinforcement, monitoring and upgrading. This is made possible through re-engineering your key business processes to be responsive to external dynamism. Our approach seeks to ensures that your business is equipped with best-in-class efficiency approaches, nimble organizational structures, training, appropriate technology, and more, depending on your needs. Organizational culture is critical, and our strategic approaches need to be complimented with corresponding interventions in human capital, if we are to jointly succeed. This can only come through a deep partnership between EFG and the Management of the specific business.
